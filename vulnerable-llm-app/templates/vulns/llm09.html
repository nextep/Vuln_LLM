⚠️ Overreliance Risks

False Confidence:

  • • AI provides confident but incorrect assessments
  • • Users skip manual verification
  • • Critical vulnerabilities get missed
  • • Security teams become complacent

Decision Automation:

  • • Automatic deployment of AI-approved code
  • • Blind trust in AI security recommendations
  • • Reduced human oversight and validation
  • • Lack of independent verification processes

🛡️ Remediation

  • Implement human-in-the-loop verification for critical decisions
  • Use AI as a supplementary tool, not a replacement for human expertise
  • Establish multi-layer review processes with independent validation
  • Regular training on AI limitations and failure modes
  • Implement confidence scoring and uncertainty quantification
  • Create fallback procedures when AI confidence is low
  • Conduct regular audits of AI-assisted decisions
  • Maintain human oversight for high-stakes scenarios
