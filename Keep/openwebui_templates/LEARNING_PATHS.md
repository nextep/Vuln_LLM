## 📋 Detailed Learning Paths

### 🔍 **Path 1: Comprehensive Security Analyst**

#### **Phase 1: Foundation Building (Weeks 1-4)**
*Goal: Master fundamental concepts across all vulnerability types*

**Week 1: Core Vulnerabilities**
- Day 1-2: LLM01 Level 1 (Prompt Injection Basics)
- Day 3-4: LLM02 Level 1 (Output Handling Fundamentals)  
- Day 5-6: LLM06 Level 1 (Information Disclosure)
- Day 7: Review and consolidation

**Week 2: System Vulnerabilities**
- Day 1-2: LLM04 Level 1 (Model DoS)
- Day 3-4: LLM05 Level 1 (Supply Chain)
- Day 5-6: LLM07 Level 1 (Plugin Security)
- Day 7: Hands-on practice lab

**Week 3: Behavioral Vulnerabilities**
- Day 1-2: LLM08 Level 1 (Excessive Agency)
- Day 3-4: LLM09 Level 1 (Overreliance)
- Day 5-6: LLM10 Level 1 (Model Theft)
- Day 7: Integration exercises

**Week 4: Data & Training Vulnerabilities**
- Day 1-3: LLM03 Level 1 (Training Data Poisoning)
- Day 4-5: Cross-vulnerability pattern recognition
- Day 6-7: Foundation assessment and gap analysis

#### **Phase 2: Skill Development (Weeks 5-8)**
*Goal: Develop intermediate technical skills*

**Week 5-6: Core Attack Techniques**
- LLM01 Level 2: Advanced prompt injection
- LLM02 Level 2: Complex output exploitation
- LLM06 Level 2: Sophisticated information extraction
- Lab: Multi-vector assessment methodology

**Week 7-8: System & Infrastructure**
- LLM04 Level 2: Complex DoS attacks
- LLM05 Level 2: Supply chain exploitation
- LLM07 Level 2: Advanced plugin attacks
- Lab: Infrastructure security assessment

#### **Phase 3: Advanced Techniques (Weeks 9-12)**
*Goal: Master expert-level attack and analysis techniques*

**Week 9-10: Behavioral Exploitation**
- LLM08 Level 2: Social engineering + technical
- LLM09 Level 2: Complex overreliance scenarios
- LLM10 Level 2: Model extraction techniques
- Lab: Behavioral security assessment

**Week 11-12: Advanced Integration**
- LLM03 Level 2-3: Training data attacks
- Cross-vulnerability attack chaining
- Advanced persistence techniques
- Lab: Comprehensive security assessment

#### **Phase 4: Professional Application (Weeks 13-16)**
*Goal: Apply skills in professional contexts*

**Week 13-14: Expert Techniques**
- Selected Level 3 challenges based on specialization
- Novel attack development
- Defense evaluation and bypass
- Research: Emerging vulnerability trends

**Week 15-16: Capstone Project**
- Design comprehensive LLM security assessment methodology
- Create professional assessment report template
- Present findings to peer review panel
- Portfolio development and certification preparation

#### **Learning Objectives by Phase**
**Phase 1**: Fundamental awareness and basic execution
**Phase 2**: Intermediate technical proficiency  
**Phase 3**: Advanced analytical and creative skills
**Phase 4**: Professional application and leadership

## 📊 Skill Prerequisites

### **Level 1 Prerequisites**
- **Technical**: Basic computer literacy
- **Security**: None required (designed for beginners)
- **LLM Knowledge**: Basic understanding helpful but not required
- **Time Commitment**: 4-6 hours per week minimum

### **Level 2 Prerequisites**
- **Technical**: Completion of Level 1 OR equivalent experience
- **Security**: Basic security awareness (can be gained through Level 1)
- **LLM Knowledge**: Understanding of LLM capabilities and limitations
- **Time Commitment**: 6-8 hours per week

### **Level 3 Prerequisites**
- **Technical**: Completion of Level 2 OR demonstrated intermediate skills
- **Security**: Intermediate security knowledge and experience
- **LLM Knowledge**: Practical experience with LLM implementation or research
- **Time Commitment**: 8-12 hours per week

### **Level 4 Prerequisites**
- **Technical**: Advanced technical proficiency (Level 3 completion)
- **Security**: Professional security experience or advanced academic study
- **LLM Knowledge**: Expert-level understanding of LLM systems
- **Time Commitment**: 10-15 hours per week
